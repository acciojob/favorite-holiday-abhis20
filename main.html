Goa


Goa is one of India's most popular beach destinations, known for its stunning coastline, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and peaceful atmosphere.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach – Famous for its nightlife and water sports.
  2. Fort Aguada – A historic fort overlooking the Arabian Sea.
  3. Old Goa Churches – Known for their beautiful architecture.
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfall – One of India's tallest waterfalls.
  5. Anjuna Market – A vibrant market full of handicrafts and art.

Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchao
  • Bebinca – A classic Goan dessert.
  • Chicken Cafreal
  • Sorpotel – A spicy traditional dish.
